Who We Are

EasyMove Logistics is a worldwide global delivery logistics company

Our focus is on simplifying complex supply chains, uncovering efficiency improvements that enable our customers to cut their inventories, reduce operating costs, and make significant short-term savings for long-term competitiveness. We work to connect and simplify our customers’ supply chains. As the global leader in shipping services, the company operates in more than 15 countries and employs roughly 15,000 people.
